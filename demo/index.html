Meisterguide

A rustic in-game companion overlay for PC games.

Press a hotkey and it floats over your game like Steam's overlay. Ask Meister, the built-in assistant, about whatever you're stuck on. It pulls answers from the game's wiki and the web, and shows you the sources it used.

  • Floats over your game — global hotkey that survives the game grabbing your keyboard.
  • Answers from the game's wiki and a web fallback, every claim backed by sources.
  • Works across more than one game, with a built-in wiki reader.
  • Windows desktop app, written in Python + PySide6.
